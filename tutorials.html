The tutorials provide an introduction into the DPS APIs. It's recommended they be read in the order below and assumes some familiarity with pub/sub terminology.

For compactness, the tutorials use the a/b/c/d topic string, where a, b, c, and d are the levels and / is the level separator, and strings for payload data. A real application would likely use more meaningful topic strings and structured payloads.
